I’m Vito, a Data Scientist at UBS in Zurich. I earned my Master’s degree from ETH Zurich, where my thesis focused on applying Graph Neural Networks to fluid dynamics reconstruction. Before that, I completed my Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Bari.

I have research experience from a short internship at IBM Research Zurich, where I worked on physics-informed losses in neural networks, and as a Research Assistant at ETH Zurich.
