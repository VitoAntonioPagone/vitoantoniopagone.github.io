I’m Vito, a Data Scientist at UBS in Zurich. I earned my Master’s degree from ETH Zurich, focusing on Machine Learning and Deep Learning applications in engineering. My thesis centered on applying Graph Neural Networks to fluid dynamics reconstruction. Before that, I completed my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Bari (IT).

I have research experience from a short internship at IBM Research Zurich, where I worked on physics-informed losses in neural networks, as well as from my roles as a Research Assistant and Machine Learning Researcher at ETH Zurich. Additionally, I completed an internship at MAN Energy Solutions in Zurich, where I worked on multiphase CFD topics.
