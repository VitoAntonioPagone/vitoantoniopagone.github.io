La Quattordici · Luxury Apartment

Web Design & Development for a beautiful stone house in the heart of Ostuni

Overview

A great friend of mine, Michele, renovated this beautiful stone house nestled in the heart of Ostuni’s old town. The apartment perfectly blends tradition with understated elegance. When Michele decided to share it with the world, I wanted the online experience to reflect that same quiet charm.

The result is La Quattordici Luxury Apartment — a modern, intuitive website designed to mirror the timeless atmosphere of the home itself. If you ever find yourself wandering through Ostuni, it’s the kind of place that invites you to stay a little longer.

Design & Technology

The website was built from scratch with a focus on clean design and mobile-first performance. It uses lightweight HTML and CSS for speed and simplicity, ensuring an effortless browsing experience on any device. The visuals emphasize space, light, and materials — echoing the textures of Ostuni’s architecture and the relaxed elegance of the home.

Every part of this project was crafted to feel authentic, calm, and true to the place it represents — technology used not to impress, but to invite.
